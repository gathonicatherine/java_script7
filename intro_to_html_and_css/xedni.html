What is the box model?

Its is used when designing and layouting a text. Essentially its a box that wraps around every html elements consisting of the Margins,Padding,Borders,Content.

Properties of a box model


Padding edges

Border edges.

Margin edges.

Content edges.

Calculate the total width of a give paragraph given

width=400px;

padding=20px;

border=2px;

margin=20px;


padding-top to bottom-20*2 + left to right-20*2=80

margin-top to bottom-20*2 + left to right-20*2=80

border-top to bottom-2*2 + left to right-2*2=8

total width = 80+80+8+400=568
